All the resources

This project wouldn't have been possible if it wasn't for the remarcable work put on the fan-made Final Space API. All the information about the series that you can see on this website is requested to their API.

Final Space is an American adult animated space opera comedy-drama television series created by Olan Rogers for TBS. The data and images are used without claim of ownership and belong to their respective owners. Final Space API is open source and uses a BSD license.

The texts from the Cover and Presentation pages were generated using the ChatGPT 4 AI model through Bing Copilot.

Finally, the Presentation video that features the season one trailer for the series was uploaded to the TBS' Official YouTube Channel. It is also used in this website without claim of ownership and belongs to their respective owners.
