What's this all about?

Welcome to our Final Space fan page! This is a class project for the “HTML and CSS - Aula 1” course at the Online University of Catalonia. We’ve used the Final Space API to bring you the most accurate and up-to-date information about the show.

Final Space is an epic animated sci-fi comedy about a spaceman named Gary who is working off a prison sentence and makes a mysterious new alien friend Mooncake whom he immediately bonds with. But what Gary doesn’t know is that his adorable new sidekick is actually wanted by the sinister Lord Commander who will stop at nothing to use Mooncake’s untapped powers for evil.

Our website is divided into several sections for your convenience. In the “Categories” section, you’ll find detailed information about the series, neatly organized by characters, locations, and episodes. Whether you’re looking for information on your favorite character or want to learn more about the show’s various locations, we’ve got you covered.

The “Presentation” section provides an in-depth overview of our website and its features. Here, you’ll find everything you need to know about how our site works and how to navigate it.

Lastly, our “Links” section contains all the documentation and sources we used to gather content for the website. Here, you’ll also find copyright attributions and other important details. We believe in giving credit where credit is due, and we’ve made every effort to acknowledge the sources of our information.

We’ve put a lot of effort into this project and we hope you enjoy exploring the universe of Final Space as much as we do!
